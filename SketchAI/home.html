SketchAI

SketchAI is a project based around machine learning. You are given five prompts and a neural network will attempt to guess what you have drawn.

Of course it will get it wrong sometimes, but we're here to help it learn!

Click on "Play" to get your prompts, or click on "Test" to mess around with the network without worrying about any game!

This project was created using Google's "Quick, Draw!" Dataset.
